Settings

Wooden knocks as the chairs land, panned to wherever they fall across the view. Whatever they land on answers underneath, quietly, until it is buried too deep to move.

Sharp leads with the crack of the strike. Mellow is the original: lower, and left to ring on. Tap either to hear it.

Drops the chairs on its own and drifts the camera around the pile, diving in among the chairs and back out. Turn it off to take the controls back.

How often demo mode sends one down. Wind it right down and they come faster than they can land. Your own taps are never held back.

How to play

Tap or press any key to send another chair down. Drag to look around, scroll to zoom into the pile.

Save writes the pile and camera to a JSON file. Load rebuilds that exact scene, welded still, so the same moment can be revisited or compared run to run.
